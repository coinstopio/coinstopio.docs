# How To Wipe KeepKey Device

Only wipe your device if you have access to your recovery sentence or you have moved all funds off your KeepKey.

1. Open your KeepKey's **settings** by clicking on the gear icon in the top left corner of the application.

2. Select **wipe device**

3. Confirm this action on your KeepKey device

  

_Note: you can also access a limited settings page directly from the PIN entry screen, where you have the option to wipe your device._
